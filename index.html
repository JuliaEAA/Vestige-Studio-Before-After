About Me

Diana Cuth - Owner & Hairdresser

At Vestige Studio, I combine years of experience, continuous training, and a passion for highlighting natural beauty. My goal is to create styles that inspire confidence while keeping hair healthy and radiant.

My journey as a hairstylist began in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, where I had the privilege of learning from some of the best professionals in the industry. Those early years shaped not only my technical skills but also my belief that hairstyling is about more than just looks. It's about making people feel seen, cared for, and comfortable in their own skin.

After moving to Denmark, I continued to grow my expertise by attending numerous advanced courses and earning diplomas in cutting, coloring, and modern techniques.
